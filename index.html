S Rank

The Dancer A.K.A

Cha Hae In

Cha Hae-In (차해인車海寅) is the Vice-Guild Master of the Hunters Guild and the sword woman of the main raiding party. She is the 9th ranked Hunter in South Korea; also being its only female S-Rank Hunter.

Hae-In was a direct participant of the 4th Jeju Island Reclamation Raid as a member of the Korean S-Rank Raid team. She possesses the unique ability of mana smell and dislikes the smell of hunters except for Sung Jin-Woo.
